A Remote Controlled Rover

The Pan and Tilt controls can be used to change the direction of the camera. The camera is fitted on to a Pan and Tilt Bracket operated by 2 Servos. The Pi server will notify you when the servo pan/tilt limit has been reached. The Movement controls can be used to move the Rover in the desired direction.
